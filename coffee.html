Tim Wendelboe Grüners gate 1 · Grünerløkka
›

Oslo's most celebrated roastery, founded by a World Barista Champion. Precise, bright filter coffees with extraordinary provenance. Heavy on Ethiopian and Kenyan lots — floral, fruit-forward, and complex. Top picks: Geisha El Vikingo, Echemo Ethiopia, and Finca Tamana.

Java Ullevålsveien 47 · Bislett

A small neighbourhood gem near Bislett. Light-roast focused, excellent single-origin filter. The Peruvian Damian Espinoza was a standout — hazelnut, stone fruit and vanilla biscuit in a very refined cup.

Kaffa Oslo — Light roast, Damian Espinoza, Cajamarca Peru5 / 5Hazelnut, stone fruit, vanilla biscuit.
