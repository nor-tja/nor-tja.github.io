My coffee notes

Tasting notes from Oslo's best specialty coffee bars. ❤️ marks a personal favourite.

On the map

The places

Tim Wendelboe Grüners gate 1

Oslo's most celebrated roastery — founded by a World Barista Champion. Expect precise, bright filter coffees with extraordinary provenance. Heavy on Ethiopian and Kenyan lots, often floral and fruit-forward. The range rewards repeat visits.

Tatmara Ethiopia 74110 — Natural 4 / 5 Floral, tropical fruit, strawberry. Slightly burnt finish.
Caballero — Honduras ❤️ Niacinamento process. 4.5/5. SL28 variety — red berries, deeply satisfying. 4.5 / 5
Geisha El Vikingo — Honduras Washed ❤️ Herbal, floral, citrus. One of the best. 5 / 5
Finca Tamana — Colombia Washed ❤️ Herbal, caramel, chocolate. A repeat favourite.
Finca Tamana Variedad — El Pital, Huila, Colombia Washed 4.5 / 5 Sweet chocolate, caramel & red fruits.
Echemo — Ethiopia Heirloom Washed ❤️ 5 / 5 Floral, white tea, stone fruits. Exceptional.
Karogoto — Kenya SL28, SL14, Batian Washed 4.5 / 5 Blackberry, hibiscus, blackcurrant. Dark chocolate, slightly heavy.
Caballero Honduras Pacamara — Washed 4.5 / 5 Winery, ripe red berries, tropical fruits.
Cielito Lindo Ihcafe90 — Honduras Washed 3.5 / 5 Forest berries, herbal, cacao.
Nacimiento El Salvador Pacas — Washed 4 / 5 Stone fruits, red berries, herbal. A little too bitter.
Los Pirineos Pacamara — Honey Winey, stone fruits, cacao nibs.
Kapsokisio — Kenya K7, SL28 & SL34 Washed 4 / 5 Rose hips, hibiscus & red berries.
Fuglen Coffee Roasters St. Halvards gate 33

Roastery-café with a cocktail bar soul. The coffee programme is serious and the vibe is relaxed Nordic. Strong on Kenyan and Peruvian lots. Several 5/5 standouts — particularly the Kenyan Ngurueri and Kiambara.

Ei Rejo — Washed ❤️ Stone fruit, raspberry, apricot.
Peru — La Chancha Washed 5 / 5 Passionfruit, pear, yellow plum.
Peru — La Mandarina 5 / 5 Orange marmalade, wild berries, white tea.
Guatemala — Valentín Carrillo Washed 4 / 5 Raisin, dark chocolate, honeycomb.
Ngurueri PB — Kenya Washed (Kiamabara, Riuru 11, SL28, SL34, K7) 5 / 5 Plum, orange, papaya.
Kiambara — Kenya, Mugaga Cooperative, Nyeri (SL28, SL34, K7, Riuru 11) Washed 5 / 5 Black tea, rosehip, blackcurrant.
Colombia Santa Barbara — Castillo Natural Anaerobic, Huila 4.5 / 5 Strawberry, mango, cocoa, funky, lemon candy.
Saime Cardenas — Costa Rica, Red Honey, West Valley 4.5 / 5 Red honey process, West Valley region.
Kaffebrenneriet Akersgata 16 (and other locations)

Oslo's most widespread specialty chain, with consistently good quality. The Kenyan lots are particular highlights — especially the Muranga Gatagua and Yirgacheffe Banko Gotiti. Some Norwegian-seasonal blends worth trying too.

La Tia — Blue, Jasmine Honey, Berry-dried 3 / 5
Sommernatt 4 / 5
Muranga — Kenya Gatagua (SL-28, SL-34, Ruiri 11) 5 / 5 Blackcurrant, pineapple & lemon balm.
Kirinyaga — Kenya Kiburi (SL-28, SL-34, Ruiri 11) Dried without pulp 4.5 / 5 Plum, lime & vanilla. Gets slightly rancid/sour toward the end.
Yirgacheffe — Ethiopia Heirloom Banko Gotiti 5 / 5 Passionfruit, jasmine & honey.
Java Ullevålsveien 47

A small neighbourhood gem near Bislett. Light-roast focused, excellent single-origin filter. The Peruvian Damian Espinoza was a standout — hazelnut, stone fruit, and vanilla biscuit in a very refined cup.

Kaffa Oslo — Light roast, Damian Espinoza, Cajamarca Peru 5 / 5 Hazelnut, stone fruit, vanilla biscuit.
Hakone Coffee Storgata 23c

Japanese-influenced café with a strong specialty programme and excellent pastries. Consistently impressive — multiple 5/5 coffees noted. The Mr. Mengeche Derso from Yirgacheffe and Finca El Placer Pink Bourbon are exceptional.

Mr. Mengeche Derso — Ethiopia Heirloom, Gedeo Yirgacheffe Natural 5 / 5
Finca El Placer — Pink Bourbon 5 / 5
Las Mishas — Organic 5 / 5
AA Gondo 4.5 / 5
Bombe 5 / 5
