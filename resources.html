100 Blocks a Day — Wait But Why

A simple but striking way to think about time. You have about 1,000 waking minutes each day — or 100 blocks of 10 minutes each. The article invites you to ask: what are you actually spending those blocks on? How many go toward creating something versus consuming? Toward other people versus yourself? Toward your future versus just today? It won't give you a system, but it might make you pause before you open your next tab.
